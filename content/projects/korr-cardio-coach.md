## Project Overview

Product housing and retail display solution for KORR's CardioCoach metabolic analyzer. The challenge was creating a professional medical device housing that could transition seamlessly from clinical environments to retail fitness stores.

## Challenge

KORR needed a solution that would:
- Protect sensitive metabolic testing equipment
- Maintain medical device aesthetics in consumer retail
- Enable easy transport between locations
- Communicate precision and scientific credibility
- Work in both gym and medical office environments

Key requirements:
- Durable powder-coated steel construction
- Integrated cable management
- Secure equipment mounting
- Clean, professional appearance
- Retail-ready graphics integration

## Solution

Developed a modular housing system with:
- Premium powder-coated steel frame
- Removable acrylic panels for equipment access
- Integrated digital graphics for branding
- Built-in cable management channels
- Rolling base for portability
- Adjustable shelf system

The design balanced medical precision with retail appeal, using clean lines and professional finishes that work in any environment. Graphics panels communicate the science behind the technology while maintaining the serious, clinical aesthetic the product requires.
